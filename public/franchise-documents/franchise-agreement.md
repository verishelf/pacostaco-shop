# Franchise Agreement

**Between:** Paco's Taco Shop Franchising LLC ("Franchisor")  
**And:** Gonzalez Restaurant Group LLC ("Franchisee")  
**Effective Date:** March 10, 2024  
**Location:** Paco's Taco Shop — Oceanside, California

---

## IMPORTANT NOTICE

This document is a **summary of executed agreement terms** for franchisee reference. The fully executed Franchise Agreement and Franchise Disclosure Document (FDD) control in the event of any conflict. This summary does not constitute legal advice.

---

## 1. Grant of Franchise

Franchisor grants Franchisee a **non-exclusive license** to operate one Paco's Taco Shop restaurant at:

**123 Fiesta Avenue, Taco Suite A, Oceanside, CA 92054**

### 1.1 Territory

Protected territory radius: **2.5 miles** from store centroid, subject to exceptions for non-traditional venues (airports, universities) as defined in Exhibit B.

---

## 2. Term & Renewal

| Item | Terms |
|------|-------|
| Initial term | 10 years from opening date |
| Renewal term | Two additional 5-year terms |
| Renewal conditions | Good standing, remodel compliance, training refresh |

---

## 3. Fees

### 3.1 Initial Fees (Paid at Signing)

- **Initial franchise fee:** $45,000
- **Training & opening support:** $12,500
- **Technology package:** $8,000

### 3.2 Ongoing Fees

| Fee | Rate | Due |
|-----|------|-----|
| Royalty | 6% of gross sales | Monthly |
| Marketing fund | 2% of gross sales | Monthly |
| Technology fee | $499/month | Monthly |
| Local co-op (optional) | Up to 1% match | Per campaign |

**Gross sales** excludes sales tax and documented refunds.

---

## 4. Franchisee Obligations

Franchisee agrees to:

1. Operate full-time through a qualified on-site manager or owner-operator
2. Use only approved suppliers, recipes, and branding
3. Participate in required training and annual conferences
4. Maintain insurance: GL $2M, workers comp, property, cyber
5. Submit monthly sales reports by the 5th business day
6. Permit inspections and mystery shops without prior notice
7. Comply with all food safety, labor, and ADA laws

---

## 5. Franchisor Support

Franchisor provides:

- Initial 3-week training program (corporate store + classroom)
- Opening team support (10 days on-site)
- Operations manual and document library (back office portal)
- Quarterly business reviews with franchise coach
- National marketing fund campaigns
- POS, inventory, and back office technology platforms

---

## 6. Transfer & Sale

Franchisee may transfer ownership with Franchisor approval. Transfer fee: **$10,000** plus buyer training fees. Buyer must complete training and sign new franchise agreement at then-current terms.

**Right of first refusal:** Franchisor may match bona fide third-party offers.

---

## 7. Default & Termination

### 7.1 Events of Default Include:

- Failure to pay royalties within 15 days of notice
- Three health inspection scores below 85 in 12 months
- Unauthorized menu or branding changes
- Abandonment of location for 5+ consecutive days

### 7.2 Remedies

Franchisor may terminate and require de-identification of location within 30 days. Post-termination non-compete: **18 months / 15 miles**.

---

## 8. Dispute Resolution

Disputes resolved by **binding arbitration** in San Diego County, CA under AAA Commercial Rules. Each party bears own legal fees unless awarded by arbitrator.
