# Opening & Closing Procedures

**Effective Date:** April 15, 2026  
**Audience:** Shift Leaders and General Managers

---

## Opening Checklist (Manager On Duty)

Complete before unlocking front doors.

### Security & Building

1. Exterior walk — check for vandalism, litter, adequate lighting
2. Disarm alarm; verify safe and office secured from prior close
3. Turn on hood, grills, rice cooker, and hot holding units
4. Verify walk-in cooler temp ≤ 41°F; freezer ≤ 0°F

### Food Prep

5. Review prep list from prior close; begin protein setup
6. First salsa batch by 8:00 AM (or 60 min before open)
7. Stock line: tortillas, chips, utensils, to-go packaging
8. Brew agua fresca; set up beverage station

### Front of House

9. Cash drawers counted ($150 bank); POS login test
10. Dining room tables, condiment bar, and restrooms inspected
11. Music and digital menu boards on
12. Unlock doors at posted opening time — **not before**

### Opening Sign-Off

- Manager initials on daily log
- Post staffing plan for daypart coverage

---

## Shift Change Handoff

Outgoing manager briefs incoming manager on:

- Current sales vs. forecast
- 86'd items or low inventory
- Equipment issues
- Guest complaints or recovery actions
- Staff call-outs or training needs

---

## Closing Checklist (Manager On Duty)

Complete after last guest served and dining room cleared.

### Cash & Admin

1. Run end-of-day POS report
2. Count drawers; complete deposit per bank schedule
3. Submit daily sales in back office portal
4. Secure coupons, gift cards, and signed recovery comps

### Food & Inventory

5. Label and date all prep; discard expired product
6. Cover and refrigerate all proteins and salsas
7. Log waste on waste sheet with reason codes
8. Note low inventory for next-day order

### Cleaning

9. Break down line; clean grill, prep tables, and cutting boards
10. Mop kitchen and dining room floors
11. Empty trash and replace liners; take cardboard to recycling
12. Wipe restrooms; restock supplies

### Security

13. Set timers on exterior lights (if applicable)
14. Arm alarm; lock all doors
15. Verify back door and office locked

### Closing Sign-Off

- Manager initials on daily log
- Set safe to time-delay if deposit held overnight

---

## Late-Night Safety

- Two employees minimum after 9:00 PM
- Walk guests to cars if requested
- No cash drops after dark without security camera coverage
- Report suspicious activity to local police non-emergency line

---

*Incomplete closing procedures are the #1 cause of health violations and shrinkage. Treat every item as mandatory.*
