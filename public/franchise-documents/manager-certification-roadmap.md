# Manager Certification Roadmap

**Effective Date:** February 10, 2026  
**Audience:** Shift Leaders, Assistant Managers, General Manager candidates

---

## Purpose

The Manager Certification Roadmap defines the competencies required to lead a Paco's Taco Shop location profitably and in brand compliance. Certification is required before:

- Independent keyholder shifts
- Solo closing with deposit responsibility
- Promotion to General Manager
- Equity partner consideration in multi-unit groups

---

## Certification Levels

### Level 1 — Shift Leader (Certified)

**Prerequisites:** 6 months crew experience, crew certification, ServSafe Food Handler

**Competencies:**

1. Open/close procedures without coach
2. Labor scheduling for single daypart
3. Cash reconciliation and deposit prep
4. Basic conflict resolution and guest recovery
5. Food safety log completion

**Assessment:** 4-hour practical evaluation + 85% written exam

**Compensation band:** $18–$22/hour (market adjusted)

---

### Level 2 — Assistant Manager

**Prerequisites:** Level 1 + 6 months as Shift Leader

**Competencies:**

1. Weekly scheduling within labor target
2. Inventory ordering and receiving
3. P&L literacy: food, labor, controllables
4. Interview and onboarding new crew
5. Local marketing event execution

**Assessment:** 30-day coaching period + GM recommendation + district manager interview

**Compensation band:** $48,000–$58,000 salary

---

### Level 3 — General Manager

**Prerequisites:** Level 2 + 12 months assistant manager OR corporate training program graduate

**Competencies:**

1. Full P&L ownership for location
2. Hiring and termination within HR policy
3. Vendor relationship management
4. Health inspection leadership (90+ score)
5. Franchise reporting and royalty submission
6. Community partnership development

**Assessment:** Business plan presentation + 90-day performance plan with franchise coach

**Compensation band:** $58,000–$72,000 salary + bonus (5–15% of base on KPI attainment)

---

## KPI Scorecard (GM Bonus Eligibility)

| KPI | Weight | Target |
|-----|--------|--------|
| Net sales vs. plan | 30% | 100% |
| Food cost % | 20% | ≤ 32% |
| Labor cost % | 20% | ≤ 30% |
| Guest rating | 15% | ≥ 4.5 |
| Health inspection | 15% | ≥ 90 |

Bonus paid quarterly upon corporate verification.

## Leadership Curriculum

### Required Courses (Corporate University)

1. **P&L for Operators** — 4 hours, online
2. **Coaching & Feedback** — 3 hours, online
3. **Conflict & Termination** — 2 hours, online (HR certified)
4. **Marketing Fundamentals** — 2 hours, online
5. **Annual Conference** — In-person, 2 days (required yearly)

---

## Development Plan Template

Every manager maintains a **90-day IDP** (Individual Development Plan):

1. **Strength to leverage** — What you do exceptionally well
2. **Skill gap** — One operational and one leadership gap
3. **Action steps** — Weekly milestones with coach check-ins
4. **Success metric** — Quantifiable outcome

Submit IDPs in the back office portal by the 15th of each quarter.

---

## Failure to Certify

Shift leaders who do not achieve Level 1 within 12 months of promotion may be reassigned to crew roles. General managers below KPI targets for two consecutive quarters enter a **Performance Improvement Plan** with franchise coach oversight.

---

## Path to Ownership

High-performing GMs may qualify for:

- **Franchisee-in-Training program** — 18-month pathway to minority equity in new unit
- **Area Developer referrals** — Priority for multi-unit candidates
- **Corporate store management** — Regional director track

Express interest to your franchise business coach.
