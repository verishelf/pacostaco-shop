# Local Store Marketing Playbook

**Effective Date:** March 28, 2026  
**Audience:** Franchise owners and designated marketing coordinators

---

## 1. Marketing Fund Overview

Franchisees contribute **2% of gross sales** to the National Marketing Fund. An additional **1% local co-op match** is available when you submit approved campaigns through the back office portal.

**Minimum campaign size for co-op:** $500  
**Maximum annual co-op per location:** $12,000

---

## 2. Grand Opening (Weeks -4 to +4)

### Pre-Opening (-4 to -1 weeks)

- **Week -4:** Permit signage, soft hiring event, social teasers
- **Week -2:** VIP preview for local businesses, schools, and media
- **Week -1:** Direct mail to 3-mile radius (corporate template #GO-2026)
- **Day 0:** Ribbon cutting, free chips & salsa for first 100 guests, live local radio remote (optional)

### Grand Opening Offers (Pre-Approved)

- BOGO Street Taco Trio (limit 1 per guest)
- 15% off catering orders placed during opening week
- Kids eat free on opening Sunday (4 PM close)

**Do not** run discounts deeper than 25% without corporate approval.

---

## 3. Community Partnerships

### High-ROI Partnership Types

1. **Youth sports teams** — Sponsor jerseys; host team night (15% donate-back)
2. **School PTA** — Fundraiser nights; 20% of tracked sales returned
3. **Local employers** — Corporate lunch drop-off program
4. **Chamber of Commerce** — Networking events and ribbon cutlists

### Partnership Tracking

Log all community events in the back office portal for co-op credit and brand compliance.

---

## 4. Catering Growth

Catering should reach **8–12% of revenue** for mature locations.

### Catering Outreach Targets

- Construction sites within 5 miles
- Medical offices and clinics
- Real estate offices (open house platters)
- Event venues for rehearsal dinners

**Catering kit:** Corporate provides pricing guide, order form, and delivery standards.

---

## 5. Digital Marketing Calendar

### Monthly Content Rhythm

| Week | Theme | Example Post |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 1 | Menu spotlight | "Al Pastor Tuesday — marinated 24 hours" |
| 2 | Behind the scenes | Prep video, salsa making |
| 3 | Community | Partner shoutout, local event |
| 4 | Promotion | Limited-time LTO or catering push |

### Paid Social Guidelines

- **Budget:** $300–$800/month recommended for stores $80K+/month sales
- **Radius:** 3–5 mile geo-target
- **Platforms:** Meta (Facebook/Instagram) primary; TikTok for Gen Z markets
- **Creative:** Use only Brandfolder-approved assets

---

## 6. Local PR

### Press Release Triggers

- Grand opening
- Anniversary (1, 5, 10 years)
- Charity milestone ($10K+ donated)
- New menu LTO tied to local event (Cinco de Mayo, etc.)

**Distribution:** Submit draft to marketing@pacostacoshop.com 10 business days before event.

---

## 7. In-Store Merchandising

### Required Elements

- Menu board compliance with current pricing
- LTO poster in front window (when active)
- Loyalty program signage at counter
- Community bulletin board (optional, corporate template)

### Seasonal Campaigns

Corporate releases quarterly LTO kits:

- **Q1:** Verde Chicken Melt
- **Q2:** Mango Habanero Shrimp Taco
- **Q3:** Smoky Birria Quesadilla
- **Q4:** Pozole Bowl (limited regions)

---

## 8. Measurement

Track monthly:

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Social engagement rate | 3%+ |
| Catering leads | 20+ per month |
| Community event ROI | 3:1 revenue to donation |
| Google review velocity | 15+ new reviews/month |
