# New Hire Training Program

**Program Length:** 14 days  
**Effective Date:** April 20, 2026  
**Certification Required Before Solo Station Work**

---

## Program Overview

Every new crew member completes structured training across **five stations** before certification. Shift leaders sign off each module in the training log (back office portal → Training).

**Goal:** Guest-ready team member in 14 calendar days or 40 training hours, whichever comes first.

---

## Week 1 — Foundations

### Day 1–2: Orientation & Culture

- Welcome to Paco's history and brand standards
- Uniform, attendance, and communication expectations
- Food safety basics (handwashing, temps, allergens)
- Tour of store zones and emergency exits
- **Sign-off:** Shift Leader

### Day 3–4: Front Counter & POS

- Guest greeting script and order accuracy
- POS navigation: modifiers, combos, discounts
- Cash handling and drawer basics
- Recovery scenarios role-play
- **Sign-off:** Shift Leader

### Day 5–7: Expo & Packaging

- Ticket reading and order staging
- Packaging standards: hot/cold separation, sauce on side
- Quality check: portion visual, garnish, ticket match
- Speed targets during simulated rush
- **Sign-off:** General Manager

---

## Week 2 — Production Stations

### Day 8–9: Prep & Cold Line

- Knife safety and cutting standards
- Salsa, guac, and cold prep batches
- Walk-in organization and FIFO dating
- Sanitizer buckets and cleaning rotation
- **Sign-off:** Shift Leader

### Day 10–11: Grill & Hot Line

- Protein cooking temps and hold times
- Tortilla warming and assembly standards
- Line setup and restocking during service
- 86 procedure and communication to FOH
- **Sign-off:** General Manager

### Day 12–13: Multi-Station Rotation

- Work live shifts with coach shadowing
- Rotate counter, expo, and one production station per day
- Minimum 2 peak-hour shifts observed
- **Sign-off:** General Manager

### Day 14: Certification Assessment

**Written quiz (80% to pass):** Food safety, portions, service standards

**Practical evaluation:**

1. Take 5 guest orders without error
2. Assemble 3 burritos to portion spec
3. Complete opening or closing task list with coach observation

**Certification issued:** Digital badge in training portal; crew pin after 30 days

## Re-Training Triggers

Mandatory re-training when:

- Food safety violation observed
- Guest complaint tied to individual performance
- 90+ days leave of absence
- Promotion to shift leader (see Manager Certification Roadmap)

---

## Trainer Responsibilities

Trainers must:

- Model brand standards at all times
- Provide daily feedback using the **Feedback Sandwich** (praise → improve → praise)
- Document progress daily — no sign-off without demonstrated competency
- Escalate struggling trainees to GM by Day 10

---

*Investing 14 days upfront prevents 14 months of operational friction.*
