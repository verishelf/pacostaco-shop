# Inventory Par Levels & Ordering Guide

**Effective Date:** May 1, 2026  
**Audience:** General Managers, Shift Leaders (inventory certified)

---

## 1. Approved Vendor Program

Franchise locations must purchase **95% of food and paper goods** through approved distributors to maintain recipe consistency and rebate eligibility.

### Primary Distributors

| Category | Vendor | Order Day | Delivery |
|----------|--------|-----------|----------|
| Proteins & produce | Sysco Paco's Program | Mon / Wed / Fri | Next day |
| Tortillas & bakery | La Tortilla Factory Direct | Tuesday | Thursday |
| Paper & chemicals | Restaurant Depot (account #PTS-8842) | As needed | Same day |
| Beverages | Coca-Cola Freestyle contract | Auto-replenish | Weekly |

**Emergency substitutions** require same-day approval from ops@pacostacoshop.com.

## 2. Par Level System

Par levels are calculated as: **(Average daily usage × lead time days) + safety stock**

Adjust pars seasonally — increase 15% for summer and local event weeks.

## 3. Receiving Standards

Upon delivery:

1. Check invoice against PO
2. Inspect for damage, pests, and temperature abuse
3. Reject any protein above 41°F on delivery
4. Date-label all items; rotate FIFO
5. Sign receiver in MarketMan within 2 hours

**Discrepancies** — Photo document and email vendor + corporate within 24 hours.

---

## 4. Waste Tracking

Track waste daily by category:

- **Spoilage** — Expired or time/temp failure
- **Prep waste** — Trimming, overproduction
- **Remake** — Guest recovery or error
- **Portion variance** — Over-portioning identified in audit

**Target total waste:** Under 2.5% of food purchases

---

## 6. Cost Control Levers

1. **Portion control** — Use scales and scoops; audit 5 tickets per shift
2. **Prep forecasting** — Use prior-year same-day sales + weather adjustment
3. **Vendor rebates** — Hit Sysco volume tiers for 2–4% annual rebate
4. **Theft prevention** — Camera coverage on walk-in; dual control on high-cost items

---

## 7. Prohibited Purchasing

The following void food cost rebates and may breach your franchise agreement:

- Non-approved protein sources
- Pre-made salsas or guacamole (except corporate-approved emergency SKUs)
- Generic paper goods not meeting brand print standards
