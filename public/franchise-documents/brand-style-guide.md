# Paco's Taco Shop Brand Style Guide

**Version:** 3.1  
**Effective Date:** May 15, 2026  
**Audience:** Franchise owners, marketing coordinators, approved vendors

---

## 1. Brand Essence

**Tagline:** *Authentic Mexican Food, Made Fresh Daily*

**Brand Personality:** Warm, bold, neighborhood-rooted, unpretentious, celebratory

**Voice:** Confident but never arrogant. We talk like the friend who knows the best taco spot in town — enthusiastic, specific, and proud of real ingredients.

---

## 2. Logo Usage

### 2.1 Primary Logo

The Paco's Taco Shop wordmark with sunburst icon is the primary logo. Use on signage, packaging, uniforms, and digital properties.

### 2.2 Clear Space

Maintain clear space equal to the height of the letter "P" on all sides.

### 2.3 Minimum Size

- **Print:** 1.25 inches wide
- **Digital:** 120 pixels wide

### 2.4 Logo Don'ts

- Do not stretch, rotate, or add effects (shadows, outlines)
- Do not change logo colors outside approved palette
- Do not place on busy photography without a solid overlay
- Do not create alternate logos without corporate approval

---

## 3. Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Taco Red | #C53030 | Primary brand, CTAs, accents |
| Taco Teal | #0F766E | Secondary, links, highlights |
| Taco Gold | #D97706 | Promotions, warmth, icons |
| Taco Cream | #FDFBF7 | Backgrounds, packaging |
| Taco Dark | #2D3748 | Body text, headers |

**Accessibility:** Maintain WCAG AA contrast (4.5:1) for all text on backgrounds.

---

## 4. Typography

### 4.1 Primary Typeface

**System UI / Sans-serif stack** for digital and print when brand fonts unavailable:

`ui-sans-serif, system-ui, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", Roboto, sans-serif`

### 4.2 Hierarchy

- **Headlines:** Bold/Black weight, Taco Dark or Taco Red
- **Subheads:** Semibold, Taco Teal
- **Body:** Regular, Taco Dark on Taco Cream

### 4.3 Approved Display Font (Signage Only)

**Montserrat Black** — licensed through corporate for exterior signage vendors.

---

## 5. Photography Standards

### 5.1 Food Photography

- Natural or warm lighting; never fluorescent
- Show steam, char, and fresh garnishes
- Include hands or shared plates to convey community
- No stock photography that misrepresents actual menu items

### 5.2 People Photography

- Reflect local community diversity
- Candid, joyful moments — families, coworkers, regulars
- Obtain signed model releases for marketing use

---

## 6. Packaging & Print

### 6.1 Approved Templates

Order printed materials through **Brandfolder → Approved Vendors**:

- Menus (dine-in, to-go, catering)
- Loyalty cards
- Window clings and table tents
- Catering boxes and stickers

### 6.2 Local Co-Branding

You may add **location address, phone, and hours** to approved templates. Do not alter logo placement or headline copy.

---

## 7. Social Media & Digital

See the **Local Store Marketing Playbook** for campaign specifics.

**Handle format:** @Pacostaco[City] (e.g., @PacostacoOceanside)

**Hashtags (approved):** #PacostacoShop #FreshDaily #TacoTime #MadeIn[City]

**Prohibited:** Competitor tags, political content, unapproved discounts, copyrighted music without license

---

## 8. Brand Approval Process

Submit local creative for approval when:

- Creating new print materials not in Brandfolder
- Partnering with influencers or local media
- Running paid ads with custom creative
- Modifying exterior signage

**Turnaround:** 3–5 business days via marketing@pacostacoshop.com

---

*Brand consistency drives trust. When guests see Paco's, they should know exactly what to expect.*
